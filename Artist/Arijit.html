BIOGRAPHY

Arjit Singh

About

Whenever we talk about a Bollywood singer who sings amazingly beautiful songs, we cannot afford to miss one name viz Arijit Singh. Be it a wedding or a romantic dinner or a party, they are simply incomplete without Arijit Singh songs. It is the hit Arijit Singh songs which became our “Muskurane Ki Wajah”. His songs make our “Shanivaar Raati” super amazing and don’t make us “Blame the night” for a great time. His melodious voice makes our “Chota Sa Fasana” a memorable one. Even in “Bekhayali”, all we remember is the best Arijit Singh songs. His songs are simply unmatchable and irreplaceable. Arijit Singh was born on 25th April 1987 in Jiaganj, Murshidabad, West Bengal.
